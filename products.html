Our Equipment Philosophy

We don't recommend equipment we don't use. Every piece of gear on this page is something we've tested, used with clients, or own ourselves.

Quality matters more than quantity. Buy once, use for years. Cheap equipment breaks and costs more in the long run.

Start with the basics: barbell, rack, bench, plates. Master the fundamentals before adding accessories.
